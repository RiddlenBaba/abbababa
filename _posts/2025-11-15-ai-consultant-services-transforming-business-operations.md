## What AI Consultation Actually Means

True AI consultation starts with understanding your business—not your current technology stack, but how value gets created, where bottlenecks occur, and what capabilities would unlock the next level of growth.

### Beyond Tool Selection
Most "AI consultants" help you choose between existing solutions. Real AI consultation designs custom approaches that fit your specific workflows, data patterns, and success metrics.

### Beyond Process Automation
While automation is often part of the solution, transformative AI consultation focuses on capability enhancement—helping humans and systems work together to achieve outcomes neither could manage alone.

### Beyond Technical Implementation
The most valuable AI consultation addresses organizational change, workflow redesign, and capability development alongside technical deployment.
